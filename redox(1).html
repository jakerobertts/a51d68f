Bio Redox

  • Oxidation is the loss of electrons from a molecule.
  • Reduction is the gain of electrons by a molecule.
  • Redox reactions involve the transfer of electrons between molecules, changing their oxidation states.
  • The electron transport chain uses redox reactions to generate ATP.
  • Cofactors such as NAD+ and FAD play essential roles in redox and shuttling electrons.
