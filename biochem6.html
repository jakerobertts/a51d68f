Biochemistry - RNA Processing and Translation - MCAT Practice Questions

What is the process in eukaryotic cells where primary transcript RNA is converted into mature RNA?
Post-transcriptional modification






What are the regions of a gene that code for proteins and ultimately exit the nucleus?
Exons






What are the segments of a DNA or RNA molecule which do not code for proteins and are cut out of the primary transcript?
Introns






What is the regulated process that results in a single gene coding for multiple proteins by selectively including or excluding certain segments of RNA?
Alternative splicing






What is the primary function of the 5' cap and poly-A tail on mature mRNA?
To protect mRNA for translation






Where does translation (protein synthesis) primarily occur in the cell?
Cytoplasm, at the ribosomes






What carries amino acids to the polypeptide chain as the mRNA is moved through the ribosome one codon at a time?
tRNAs






In prokaryotic translation, which sequence does the 30S ribosome subunit attach to and scan for a start codon?
Shine-Delgarno sequence






What is a polycistronic gene, which is characteristic of prokaryotic mRNA?
A segment that encodes several proteins






The three steps in translation, in their correct order, are:
Initiation, Elongation, Termination






In translation elongation, the new tRNA enters the ribosome at which site?
A site






In translation, what will occur when the codon in the A site is a stop codon?
Termination






What are modifications that occur to a protein after it has been fully formed in translation?
Post-translational modifications (PTMs)






How many chromosome(s) do prokaryotes typically have?
One circular chromosome






What is one half of a duplicated chromosome, where one is an identical copy of the other?
Chromatid






A Svedberg (S) unit is a measure of:
Sedimentation time






What is the approximate Svedberg unit (S) for a complete prokaryotic ribosome?
70 S






What is the approximate Svedberg unit (S) for a complete eukaryotic ribosome?
80 S






What is a deoxyribonucleic acid molecule with part or all of the genetic material of an organism?
Chromosome






In the ribosome, the correct order of sites a tRNA moves through before leaving is:
A-P-E
