How is this calculated?

The study plan is based on the total number of questions you need to review - nearly 2000 questions - and the number of days left until your exam. Retention of information is not effective if it is reviewed over a single session. This plan divides the total questions into three equal steps, with each step representing the number of times you have reviewed the material. The ideal number of questions per day is calculated to ensure you cover all material before the exam.
