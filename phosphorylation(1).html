Phosphorylation & Energy Transfer

Types of Phosphorylation

  • Substrate-level phosphorylation involves direct transfer of a phosphate group from a high-energy compound to ADP.
  • Oxidative phosphorylation produces ATP using energy from the electron transport chain.
  • Photophosphorylation generates ATP using light energy in photosynthesis.

Energy Transfer Molecules

  • ATP is the primary energy currency of cells, storing energy in phosphate bonds.
  • Creatine phosphate is a molecule that serves as an energy reserve in muscle cells.
  • GTP is a nucleotide used in protein synthesis and signal transduction.
