Half-reactions, Flavoproteins, & Electron Carriers

  • Half-reactions are used to balance redox reactions by separating the oxidation and reduction processes, allowing for easier analysis of electron transfer.
  • Flavoproteins are a class of proteins that contain a flavin group and play a key role in various biological redox reactions.
  • Electron carriers are crucial for transferring electrons in metabolic pathways.
