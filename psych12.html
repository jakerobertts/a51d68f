The unwritten, unofficial, and often unintended lessons, values, and perspectives that students learn in school are referred to as the:
Hidden Curriculum






The weakening of social and political power of religious organizations as religious involvement declines is known as:
Secularization






A form of government in which power is vested in the people and exercised directly by them or by their elected agents under a free electoral system is a:
Democracy






A form of government advocating class warfare, leading to public ownership of property and payment based on abilities and needs is:
Communism






A form of government ruled by a king, queen, or emperor is a:
Monarchy






An economic system based on private ownership of production means, operating for profit, is:
Capitalism






The process of defining and treating conditions as medical issues, leading to medical intervention, is called:
Medicalization






Actions taken by individuals who perceive themselves as ill, aiming for recovery, are described as:
Sick-role Behavior






The branch of epidemiology studying the impact of social structures on health outcomes is:
Social Epidemiology






Treating a person or animal as an object or thing is defined as:
Objectification
