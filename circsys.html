Oxygen Transport & Hemoglobin

Which cell type primarily carries oxygen in the bloodstream?
Erythrocytes






_____________________________________________________________________________
Hemoglobin is composed of how many polypeptide chains, and where does oxygen bind?
4 chains; binds to iron in heme






_____________________________________________________________________________
What describes "cooperative binding" of O₂ to hemoglobin?
Binding of one O₂ molecule makes it easier for other O₂ molecules to bind






_____________________________________________________________________________
The hemoglobin dissociation curve is typically:
Sigmoidal






_____________________________________________________________________________
What is the effect of 2,3-BPG on hemoglobin's affinity for oxygen?
It decreases affinity (shifts curve right)






_____________________________________________________________________________
Which of the following conditions promotes release of O₂ from hemoglobin (Bohr effect)?
Low pH, high CO₂






_____________________________________________________________________________
Which molecule has a hyperbolic (not sigmoidal) O₂ dissociation curve and serves as an oxygen reservoir in muscle?
Myoglobin






_____________________________________________________________________________
Why does fetal hemoglobin (HbF) have a higher affinity for oxygen compared to adult hemoglobin?
It binds 2,3-BPG less strongly






_____________________________________________________________________________
In sickle cell anemia, which amino acid substitution occurs in hemoglobin?
Glu → Val

_____________________________________________________________________________

_____________________________________________________________________________

_____________________________________________________________________________
Which statement is true regarding arteries and veins?
Arteries carry blood away from the heart; veins carry blood toward the heart






_____________________________________________________________________________
