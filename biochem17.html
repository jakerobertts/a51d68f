Biochemistry - Glycogen Metabolism - MCAT Practice Questions

What is glycogenesis?
The process of producing glycogen from glucose






Where does glycogenesis primarily occur?
Liver and muscles






Which glycolysis intermediate feeds into glycogenesis?
Glucose 6-phosphate






Which enzyme creates the main chain of glycogen by forming α-1,4 glycosidic bonds?
Glycogen synthase






Which enzyme creates branches in glycogen by forming α-1,6 glycosidic bonds?
Branching enzyme






What is glycogenolysis?
The process of breaking down stored glycogen






Which enzyme removes single glucose molecules from glycogen main chains by breaking α-1,4 glycosidic bonds?
Glycogen phosphorylase






Which enzyme breaks branches off glycogen by hydrolyzing α-1,6 glycosidic bonds?
Debranching enzyme






What type of glycosidic bonds form the main chain of glycogen?
α-1,4 glycosidic bonds






What type of glycosidic bonds form the branch points in glycogen?
α-1,6 glycosidic bonds
