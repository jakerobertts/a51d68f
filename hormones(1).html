Hormones & the Endocrine System

  • Hormones are produced by the endocrine system and travel through the bloodstream to target organs.
  • Hormones can be classified into three main types: peptide hormones, steroid hormones, and Tyrosine-derived hormones.
  • Hormones regulate various physiological processes, including metabolism, growth, and reproduction.
  • Hormone deficiency or excess can lead to disorders such as diabetes, thyroid disease, and adrenal insufficiency.
