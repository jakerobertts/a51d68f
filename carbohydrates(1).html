Carbohydrates

Classification

  • Monosaccharides are the simplest carbohydrates.
  • Disaccharides consist of two monosaccharides: sucrose, lactose, and maltose.
  • Polysaccharides include starch, glycogen, and cellulose which cannot be digested by humans.

Structure and Function

  • Aldoses contain an aldehyde group, while ketoses contain a ketone group.
