About This Site

Hi everyone! My name is Jake and I am the creator of Percentyle. I am a biomedical engineering student at Arizona State University. I graduated SUNY Geneseo with a degree in biology in 2023, but I wanted to pursue more options in biology and the medical field. As I was pursuing these options, I started studying for the MCAT in May of 2025. I created the website primarily to help students study for the MCAT. I found it quite difficult to study for the MCAT on my own due to expensive resources, so I wanted to create a platform that would make studying more accessible and affordable for everyone using tools and prior knowledge I obtained with my bachelor's in biology. This site offers the basic combination of Skills 1 and 2 required for the MCAT. Skill 1 is primarily content knowledge, and Skill 2 is problem-solving based on that knowledge. My site provides the necessary resources and tools to help students succeed in their MCAT preparation. Please feel free to contact me through this email below if you have any questions. Good luck studying!
