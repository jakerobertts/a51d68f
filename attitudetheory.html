Comprehensive MCAT Checklist - Attitude Theories

Which of the following is NOT one of the three main components of an attitude?
Physical






Which example best represents the affective component of attitude?
Feeling happy when you see a puppy






Which best describes the theory of planned behavior?
Behavior is influenced by attitudes, what others think, and how easy it is to perform the behavior.






Which example best illustrates the attitude to behavior process model?
Being told you will fail a class, so you study more






A person isn't convinced to get a different brand of shoes not because of the seller's reasoning, but because they were sluggish and sounded unenthusiastic. Which principle does this illustrate?
Elaboration likelihood model - peripheral route






Which of the following regarding reciprocal determinism is false?
The flow of social, cognitive, and environmental factors must be influenced in a one-way fashion






Which of the following contributes to learned helplessness?
Perception of lack of control






According to the tyranny of choice, which of the following would impair decision making?
Having too many options






Which best describes the difference between internal and external locus of control?
Internal locus means you control your fate; external means outside forces control your fate.
